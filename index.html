Did you know? Over 1 million marine animals are dying because of water pollution!

We use a lot of plastic in our daily lives, such as toothbrushes, water bottles, ballpoint pens, plastic bags, etc.
These plastics come together and a total of 300 million are thrown away each year. Very few of these plastics are recycled. Most of the non-recyclable plastic is buried underground or leaked into the sea through channels such as rivers.
This leaked plastic severely damages the marine ecosystem.
